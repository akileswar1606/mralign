Features & Specifications

The Modular Workstation Platform.

Every mounting option, material, and connectivity feature behind the Mr.Align industrial laptop tray mount system.

Magnetic Laptop Holder for Electrical Panels

A switchable magnet laptop mount that locks flat against any panel door, with a rotary magnetic mounting base for full-range positioning without drilling or clamping hardware.

Heavy Duty C-Clamp Mount

Bites onto rails, tables, and scaffolding edges up to 6cm thick — the same heavy duty C clamp laptop mount used on construction and EPC sites.

Industrial Tripod Laptop Stand

A freestanding option for open floors with no panel or rail nearby — full three-point stability on uneven ground.

Articulating Arm, No Sag

Friction-locked joints hold position under load — an articulating arm laptop mount that doesn't drift or sag through a full shift of use.

Matte Black Anodized Aluminum

The full frame is built from aerospace grade aluminum laptop stand stock, anodized matte black for scratch and corrosion resistance in harsh plant environments.

Quick Release Laptop Tray

A quick release laptop tray, industrial rated, lets you swap between mount types in seconds without tools.

Internal Cable Routing

An internal cable routing laptop stand design keeps power and data cables inside the arm, not dangling loose across the panel.

Industrial Tablet Holder, Quick Release

Swap the laptop tray for the optional industrial tablet holder with the same quick-release interface.

The optional Mr.Align Dock is an integrated USB-C docking hub stand accessory — Ethernet, HDMI, storage, and power all live on the mount itself.

Pass-Through Ethernet

A pass-through ethernet laptop stand connection means one cable run from the panel, not a separate dongle hanging off the laptop.

Built-In HDMI
